# Question Package — Oil Ceasefire Bet
*Created: 2026-04-09 | Status: Open — awaiting research gate*

---

## The Question

Who placed the $950 million bearish oil bet on April 7, 2026, and did they have advance knowledge of Trump's Iran ceasefire announcement?

---

## What Prompted It

A Perplexity bookmark surfaced a Barchart story reporting that 8,600 lots of Brent and WTI crude futures were sold at 19:45 GMT on April 7 — roughly 2.5 hours before Trump announced a two-week ceasefire with Iran at ~22:30 GMT. Oil fell 15% the following day. The trade was executed in an unusually quiet window for that scale. The pattern matches a prior trade on March 22, 2026, which preceded Trump's delay of Iran energy strikes by 15 minutes.

---

## Initial Evidence

- **The April 7 trade:** 6,200 Brent lots + 2,400 WTI lots sold at 19:45 GMT. Notional value ~$950M. Executed via block sale / sweeping orders across NYMEX and ICE during low-volume hours. Confirmed by Reuters/LSEG data.
- **The March 22 pattern:** ~$500–580M oil short + $1.5B S&P 500 E-mini buy + $192M WTI short (~$2.28B total) executed 15 minutes before Trump delayed Iran energy strikes. Same structural signature.
- **Political response:** Rep. Ritchie Torres (D-NY) called for SEC/CFTC investigation on April 8. Agencies have not responded publicly.
- **Polymarket parallel:** 4 new wallets profited ~$600K on ceasefire outcome via prediction markets — separate but related.
- **Characterization:** Reuters/LSEG described volume and timing as "entirely atypical." One analyst: "If you know, these are exactly the trades you would make."

## Suggested Angles

1. **Identity of the trader** — Who executed this? A London broker, a fund, a politically connected entity? CFTC has the trade records. FOIA is the path.
2. **The recurring pattern** — Two trades, same structural signature, both preceding Trump Iran policy moves. Is this a pattern or coincidence? Statistical analysis of the timing.
3. **The information chain** — Who knew about the ceasefire before 22:30 GMT? Backtrack the diplomatic timeline. Who was in the room?
4. **Regulatory non-response** — Torres called for a probe on April 8. Has the SEC/CFTC responded? What jurisdiction applies to futures insider trading?
5. **Polymarket parallel** — The 4 wallets that profited on the ceasefire prediction. Are they connected to the futures trades? Same information source?
