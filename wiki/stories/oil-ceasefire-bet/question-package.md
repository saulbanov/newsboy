# Question Package — Oil/Trump Trades Pattern

## Central development sentence

Traders have repeatedly positioned ahead of Trump foreign policy announcements since January 2025, profiting as markets moved on what appears to be advance knowledge of non-public White House decisions.

## Source angle

From intake analysis, Story 2: The full pattern of Trump-announcement-adjacent commodity and equity trades — including but extending beyond the two documented oil trades — reveals a systematic signal that has transferred hundreds of millions of dollars from uninformed traders to whoever holds advance knowledge of White House decisions.

## Key established facts

- April 8, 2026: $950M bearish oil bet placed ~3 hours before Trump's Iran ceasefire announcement; oil fell 15–19%
- March 23, 2026: $580M oil futures sale placed ~15 minutes before Trump's Truth Social post about "productive conversations" with Iran; oil fell 14%
- WSJ documented a broader pattern of well-timed trades across multiple Trump policy surprises
- Senator Murphy called the timing "mindblowing corruption"; White House denied profiteering
- CFTC, CME, ICE — the regulatory and exchange surveillance apparatus — absent from all coverage to date

## Reporting question

How broad and systematic is the pattern of trades preceding Trump foreign policy announcements, and does it extend beyond oil futures to other asset classes?
